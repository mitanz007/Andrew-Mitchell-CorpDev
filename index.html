About

I am a Strategy and Corporate Development Executive who cuts through the noise to get deals done via a hands-on approach. With a track record of $32bn+ in closed transaction value, I help organisations navigate complex mergers, acquisitions, and divestments working alongside business teams and functional advisors.

My experience spans the full deal lifecycle - from origination and financial modelling to due diligence and integration - across highly regulated and capital-intensive sectors:

  • Energy & Fuels (Ampol ASX:ALD): Leading transition strategies and supply chain optimisation.
  • Global Infrastructure (Jacobs NYSE:J): Managing international M&A and growth strategies across engineering and technical services markets.
  • FMCG/Retail (Coca-Cola NYSE/LSE:CCEP): Driving competitive advantage in fast-moving consumer environments.
